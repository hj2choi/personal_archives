Jungle (or Dou Shou Qi in Chinese) is a traditional Chinese broad game which mimics the law of jungle. It is played on a 7 x 9 board
There are 2 winning conditions:
(1) The happy (or less sad) ending: Move an animal piece onto a special landscape called “Cave” on the opposite side of the board
(2) The sad ending: Kill all the opponents

One chess piece can be moved each turn. Use Up(),Down(),Right(),Left() command with right button of the mouse.
dragging piece is NOT allowed.
